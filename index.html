Our Self-Sustainability Model

The model was coined by author Mthabisi Andy Dube in Kwerekwere. It outlines the four stages that every individual needs to go through in life for them to achieve self-sustainability.

The model argues that if you know the four critical elements of yourself and your environment then you are on the way to being self-sustainable.

The Four Critical Elements

Who

Knowledge of yourself. This includes your current values and goals for the future.

How

The power of your choices and how the decisions you make affect you.

Why

The direct and indirect influences that led to you being the way you are.

What

Whether or not the things you do every day align to your betterment and life’s purpose.
